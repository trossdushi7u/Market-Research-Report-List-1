<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Flooring (LVT) is a popular flooring option known for its durability and aesthetic appeal, available in various types. Dry-Back LVT is glued directly to the subfloor, ensuring stability. Loose-Lay LVT is easy to install without adhesive, allowing for easy removal and replacement. Stone Plastic Composite (SPC) features a rigid core for enhanced strength and water resistance, while Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) combines wood and plastic for added comfort and insulation. Each type caters to different needs and installation preferences.</p></p>
