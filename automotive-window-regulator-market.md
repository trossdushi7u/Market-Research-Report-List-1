<p><p>The automotive window regulator market can be categorized into manual and automated systems. In contrast, automated window regulators utilize electric motors to control window movement, offering convenience and ease of use. They are prevalent in modern vehicles, often featuring additional functions like one-touch operation and anti-pinch safety mechanisms, enhancing user experience and safety.</p></p>
