<p><p>The guidewires market primarily consists of straight and J-shaped guidewires, each serving specific medical applications. J-shaped guidewires feature a curved tip that aids in easier navigation around vascular structures, reducing the risk of vessel trauma. Both types are essential for minimally invasive surgeries, facilitating the accurate delivery of catheters and other instruments in diagnostic and therapeutic procedures across the healthcare sector.</p></p>
