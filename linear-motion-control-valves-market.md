Ball valves provide quick operation and excellent sealing, suitable for high-pressure systems. Diaphragm valves offer tight shut-off capabilities and are used in sanitary and corrosive environments. Other valve types may include globe valves and pinch valves, each designed for specific pressure and flow requirements, ensuring efficient fluid management across industries.</p></p>
