<p><ul><li>Halogen Flame</li><li>Halogen Free Flame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polypropylene Granule market is divided into two main types: Halogen Flame and Halogen Free Flame retardants. Halogen Flame retardants contain halogen elements, such as chlorine or bromine, which effectively reduce flammability but can release toxic gases when burned. Conversely, Halogen Free Flame retardants utilize alternative compounds, such as phosphorus or nitrogen, offering a safer profile by eliminating harmful emissions. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing the safety and performance of polypropylene in various applications.</p></p>
