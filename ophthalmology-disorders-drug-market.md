<p><p>Ophthalmology disorders drug market encompasses three main types of administration: oral, injection, and external use. Injections typically deliver biologics or steroids directly into the eye for conditions like retinal diseases or uveitis, ensuring targeted action. External use drugs include topical solutions and ointments, commonly prescribed for surface-level issues like dry eye or infections, allowing for localized treatment with minimal systemic exposure.</p></p>
