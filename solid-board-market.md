<p><p>The solid board market is primarily divided into two types: bleached solid board and unbleached solid board. Bleached solid board is produced from wood fibers that have undergone bleaching processes, resulting in a bright white appearance ideal for high-quality packaging and printing applications. In contrast, unbleached solid board retains its natural color, often featuring a brownish tone, and is typically used for less demanding applications like shipping boxes and eco-friendly packaging. Both types serve distinct market needs based on aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
