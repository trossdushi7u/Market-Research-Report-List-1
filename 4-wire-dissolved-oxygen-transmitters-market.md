<p><p>The 4-Wire Dissolved Oxygen Transmitter market is categorized into three types: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel. Single Channel transmitters monitor one location for dissolved oxygen levels, ideal for simple applications. Dual Channel models can simultaneously measure oxygen levels in two different points, providing enhanced monitoring flexibility. Multi Channel transmitters offer comprehensive capabilities, allowing for the measurement of multiple locations at once, making them suitable for complex systems and extensive water quality monitoring requirements.</p></p>
