<p><p>Outer packaging machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal markets based on their design and application. Vertical outer packaging machines typically operate with packaging materials fed from the top, ideal for products like granules or powders. In contrast, horizontal machines use a side feeding method, suitable for larger or bulk items such as boxes or cartons. Each type caters to different industry needs, optimizing packaging efficiency and speed while ensuring product integrity during transit and storage.</p></p>
