<p><p>The Bridge Deflection Instrument market comprises two primary types: dynamic and static instruments. Dynamic Bridge Deflection Instruments measure real-time deflections caused by live loads, environmental factors, and vibrations, providing insights into structural behavior during usage. In contrast, Static Bridge Deflection Instruments assess fixed deflections and deformations under static loads, focusing on long-term stability and integrity. Both types are essential for ensuring bridge safety and performance, enabling engineers to monitor, analyze, and maintain critical infrastructure effectively.</p></p>
