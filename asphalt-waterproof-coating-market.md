<p><p>The asphalt waterproof coating market is primarily divided into two types: liquid asphalt waterproof coating and dry asphalt waterproof coating. Liquid asphalt waterproof coatings are applied as a fluid, forming a seamless barrier upon drying, making them ideal for complex surfaces and extensive waterproofing needs. In contrast, dry asphalt waterproof coatings come in a solid form, often used for specific applications such as membranes or sheets. Both types offer effective protection against water infiltration and are essential in construction and maintenance projects.</p></p>
