<p><p>The Direct-Coat IR Glazing market is characterized by two primary types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer IR glazing consists of a single layer of coating that provides infrared reflectance and energy efficiency, making it simpler and cost-effective. On the other hand, multilayer IR glazing involves multiple coatings that enhance thermal performance and control light spectrum absorption more effectively. This complexity allows for superior energy management and versatility in applications, catering to both residential and commercial needs.</p></p>
