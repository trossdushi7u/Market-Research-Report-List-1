<p><p>The ring splints market includes specialized splints designed to support finger conditions. Swan neck splints are used to stabilize the proximal interphalangeal joint in hyperextension, addressing deformities associated with rheumatoid arthritis. Boutonniere splints provide support for the PIP joint, aiding recovery from injuries that cause bending dysfunction. Oval-8 finger splints are versatile, made of thermoplastic material, and designed to treat various finger conditions by providing adjustable support. Collectively, these splints enhance functionality and comfort for individuals with finger deformities or injuries.</p></p>
