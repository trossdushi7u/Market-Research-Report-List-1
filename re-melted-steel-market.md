<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Re-melted Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro Slag Remelting (ESR) Process</li><li>Vacuum Arc Remelting (VAR) Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The re-melted steel market primarily features two key processes: Electro Slag Remelting (ESR) and Vacuum Arc Remelting (VAR). The ESR process involves melting steel through an electrical arc and slag, producing high-quality steel with minimal impurities. In contrast, the VAR process uses a vacuum environment to melt steel via an electric arc, preventing contamination and allowing precise control over composition. Both methods aim to enhance the properties of steel, making them ideal for critical applications in aerospace, automotive, and other high-performance industries.</p></p>
