<p><strong>The NDT Weld Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automated Ultrasonic Testing</li><li>Magnetic Particle Testing</li><li>Radiographic Testing</li><li>Penetrant Testing</li><li>Visual Testing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NDT weld inspection market encompasses various techniques to evaluate weld integrity. Automated Ultrasonic Testing (AUT) uses sound waves to detect defects. Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT) identifies surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials. Radiographic Testing (RT) uses X-rays or gamma rays for internal inspection. Penetrant Testing (PT) reveals surface cracks with dye or fluorescent materials. Visual Testing (VT) involves direct observation to assess weld quality. The "Others" category includes emerging methods and specialized techniques not covered by the primary types.</p></p>
