<p><p>The Cyclonic Air Classifier market encompasses two primary types: wet classifiers and dry classifiers. Wet classifiers utilize water to separate particles based on size and density, commonly employed in mineral processing and wastewater treatment. In contrast, dry classifiers operate without water, using air to achieve separation, making them ideal for materials sensitive to moisture, such as powders and granules. Both types are essential in various industries for enhancing material purity and achieving desired particle size distribution.</p></p>
