<p><strong>The Colonoscopy Bowel Preparation Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isosmotic Agents</li><li>Hyposmotic Agents</li><li>Hyperosmotic Agents</li><li>Combination Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colonoscopy bowel preparation drugs market comprises various agents that help clear the bowel for optimal visualization during the procedure. Isosmotic agents maintain fluid balance, minimizing electrolyte shifts and are often preferred for patient comfort. Hyposmotic agents draw water into the bowel, promoting effective cleansing, while hyperosmotic agents work by attracting water from surrounding tissues. Combination agents utilize multiple mechanisms for enhanced efficacy and patient tolerance, offering flexible options suited to individual preparation needs and preferences.</p></p>
