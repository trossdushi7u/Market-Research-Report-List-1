<p><p>The truck camshaft market is segmented into three main types: cast camshafts, assembled camshafts, and forged camshafts. Cast camshafts are produced by pouring molten metal into molds, offering cost-effectiveness and good precision. Assembled camshafts are constructed by combining multiple components, allowing for customization and enhanced performance. Forged camshafts, created through high-pressure shaping of metal, provide superior strength and durability, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Each type caters to specific performance needs and manufacturing requirements in the trucking industry.</p></p>
