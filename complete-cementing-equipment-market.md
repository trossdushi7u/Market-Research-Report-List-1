<p><p>Complete cementing equipment can be categorized into three types based on their mounting styles. Car-mounted equipment is designed for mobility, allowing easy transport on various terrains, making it ideal for remote locations. Skid-mounted equipment is stationary, providing enhanced stability and is typically used in fixed installations. Drag-mounted equipment offers a balance between mobility and stability, capable of being towed to different sites while maintaining a robust platform for cementing operations. Each type is tailored to specific operational needs in the cementing process.</p></p>
