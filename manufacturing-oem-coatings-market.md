<p><p>The Manufacturing (OEM) Coatings market includes various types of coatings tailored for original equipment manufacturers. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, featuring low volatile organic compounds and quick drying times. Solvent-based coatings offer excellent durability and adhesion but may have higher emissions. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and cured under heat, providing a tough finish with minimal waste. Radiation-curable coatings utilize energy sources like UV light, allowing rapid curing with lower emissions, making them efficient and sustainable.</p></p>
