<p><ul><li>ST Connectors</li><li>FC Connectors</li><li>LC Connectors</li><li>MT-RJ Connectors</li><li>SC Connectors</li><li>MU Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic interconnects market comprises various connector types, each serving specific applications. ST connectors feature a bayonet coupling mechanism for secure connections. FC connectors use a screw-on design to minimize signal loss. LC connectors are compact, ideal for high-density applications. MT-RJ connectors offer a small form factor with duplex capability. SC connectors provide reliable performance with a push-pull design, while MU connectors, designed for high-density environments, offer low insertion loss. These connectors facilitate efficient data transmission across telecommunications and networking systems.</p></p>
