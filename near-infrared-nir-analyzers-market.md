<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) analyzers market is segmented primarily into two types: FT-NIR (Fourier Transform NIR) and other technologies such as AOTF (Acousto-Optic Tunable Filter) and filter-based systems. FT-NIR uses interferometry to analyze samples, providing high sensitivity and rapid results. In contrast, AOTF and filter-based systems offer flexibility and simplicity, allowing for specific wavelength tuning. Each type caters to different applications in sectors like food, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture, ensuring precise chemical composition analysis.</p></p>
