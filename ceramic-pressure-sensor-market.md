<p><p>The Ceramic Pressure Sensor market is categorized into two main types based on output signals: Simulation Output Signals and Digital Output Signals. Simulation output signals provide continuous voltage or current that varies with pressure changes, allowing for precise analog readings. In contrast, Digital Output Signals offer binary data, typically representing pressure readings in a digital format, facilitating easier integration with digital systems and improved accuracy. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and process control.</p></p>
