<p><p>The milk powder analyzing device market comprises three main types: Ultrasonic, NIR (Near-Infrared), and Others. Ultrasonic devices utilize sound waves to assess characteristics like density and moisture content, offering non-destructive testing. NIR technology employs infrared light to analyze composition, measuring fat, protein, and other components rapidly without chemical reagents. The "Others" category may include techniques such as microwave sensing or laser methods, catering to specific needs in quality control and product development, ensuring optimal milk powder standards.</p></p>
