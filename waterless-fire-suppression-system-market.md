<p><p>The waterless fire suppression system market comprises several key types. The aerosol fire suppression system utilizes a fine aerosol mist to quickly extinguish flames, effective in confined spaces. Inert gas fire suppression systems deploy gases like nitrogen or argon to displace oxygen and suppress fire without water damage. Dry powder fire suppression systems use various powdered agents to smother flames, suitable for flammable liquids and gases. Additionally, other systems may include foam or chemical agents tailored for specific applications and environments.</p></p>
