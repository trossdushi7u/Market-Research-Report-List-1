<p><p>The vacuum shut-off valve market is categorized into three main types based on their operational vacuum levels. Low vacuum valves manage pressures up to 1 mbar, suitable for general applications. High vacuum valves operate effectively in the range of 10^-3 to 10^-7 mbar, ideal for processes requiring more precise control. Ultra-high vacuum valves are designed for pressures below 10^-7 mbar, essential for advanced scientific research and semiconductor manufacturing where minimal contamination is crucial. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
