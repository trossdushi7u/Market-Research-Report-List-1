<p><p>The dry sewage pumps market can be categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for straightforward applications, efficiently moving sewage over shorter distances with a simple impeller setup. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers that enable them to achieve higher pressures, making them suitable for lifting sewage to greater heights or over longer distances. Each type serves distinct operational needs, influencing their selection based on application requirements in sewage and wastewater management.</p></p>
