<p><p>The multifunction surface planer market consists of two main types: vertical and horizontal surface planers. Vertical surface planers are designed for vertical alignment of materials, making them ideal for tasks that require precision in thickness and flatness on large boards. In contrast, horizontal surface planers are configured for horizontal material handling, allowing for efficient planing of wide surfaces. Both types cater to various industrial applications, offering versatility in woodworking, metalworking, and other material processing fields.</p></p>
