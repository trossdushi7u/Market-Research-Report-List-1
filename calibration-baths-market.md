<p><p>Calibration baths are essential tools used for temperature calibration in various industries. The market primarily consists of two types: dry-block and liquid calibration baths. </p><p>Dry-block calibrators utilize solid blocks that generate controlled temperatures, offering portability and rapid temperature change. Conversely, liquid calibration baths employ fluids for enhanced thermal conductivity and stability, making them ideal for more precise measurements. Both types serve diverse applications, ensuring accurate calibration of temperature-sensing instruments across scientific, industrial, and laboratory settings.</p></p>
