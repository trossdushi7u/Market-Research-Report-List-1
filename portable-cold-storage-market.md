<p><p>The Portable Cold Storage Market encompasses three main types: Walk-In Coolers, Refrigerated Containers, and Refrigerated Trailers. Walk-In Coolers are large, insulated units used for storing perishable goods in various settings like restaurants or warehouses. Refrigerated Containers are portable units that can be transported via truck or ship, making them ideal for shipping food and pharmaceuticals. Refrigerated Trailers are mobile refrigerated systems that transport goods over land, providing flexibility and maintaining temperature control during transit. Each type meets specific cold storage needs across different industries.</p></p>
