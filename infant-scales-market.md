<p><p>The infant scales market comprises two primary types: electronic and mechanical scales. Electronic infant scales are equipped with digital displays and sensors, providing precise weight measurements and often features like memory storage and automatic shut-off. Conversely, mechanical infant scales utilize a traditional balance mechanism, relying on a spring and dial system for measurement. While electronic scales offer greater accuracy and ease of use, mechanical scales are typically more durable and do not require batteries, appealing to specific consumer preferences.</p></p>
