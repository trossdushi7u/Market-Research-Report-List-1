<p><p>The Fiber-coupled Electro-optical Light Modulators market comprises two primary types: Electro-optical Intensity Modulators and Electro-optical Phase Modulators. Intensity modulators control the light's amplitude, facilitating applications in telecommunications and signal processing, where varying signal strength is crucial. In contrast, phase modulators manipulate the light's phase, enabling advanced communication techniques like phase-shift keying. Both types enhance optical signal transmission in fiber optic systems, improving bandwidth and data rates, and play vital roles in modern photonic applications.</p></p>
