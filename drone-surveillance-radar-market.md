<p><strong>The Drone Surveillance Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSR(Primary Surveillance Radar)</li><li>SSR(Secondary Surveillance Radar)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drone surveillance radar is categorized into two main types: Primary Surveillance Radar (PSR) and Secondary Surveillance Radar (SSR). PSR detects and tracks objects based on reflected radio waves, providing information on the presence and location of drones in the airspace. In contrast, SSR relies on transponders aboard the drones, which send back specific information such as altitude and identification, enhancing situational awareness. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety, managing air traffic, and conducting reconnaissance in various applications.</p></p>
