<p><p>5G technology encompasses several market types, including Software-Defined Networking (SDN), Network Functions Virtualization (NFV), and Multi-Access Edge Computing (MEC). SDN focuses on centralized network management, enabling dynamic adjustments to network resources. NFV allows for virtualizing network services, making it easier to deploy and scale functions without relying on specific hardware. MEC brings computing capabilities closer to the end-users, reducing latency and enhancing performance. Together, these innovations drive the efficiency and flexibility necessary for advanced 5G applications.</p></p>
