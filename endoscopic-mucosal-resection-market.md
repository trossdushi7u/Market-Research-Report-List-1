<p><strong>The Endoscopic Mucosal Resection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lift-and-Cut Technique</li><li>Suck-and-Cut Technique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endoscopic Mucosal Resection (EMR) involves techniques for excising abnormal tissue from the gastrointestinal tract. The Lift-and-Cut Technique involves injecting a solution beneath the lesion to elevate it, allowing for a clearer margin during resection, facilitating complete removal. The Suck-and-Cut Technique, on the other hand, utilizes suction to draw the lesion into a cap and then excises it, ensuring precision and reduced risk of perforation. Both techniques are pivotal in minimally invasive surgeries, enhancing patient outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
