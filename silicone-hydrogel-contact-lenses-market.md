<p><strong>The Silicone Hydrogel Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Form</li><li>Toric Form</li><li>Multifocal Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone hydrogel contact lenses are categorized into three main types: spherical, toric, and multifocal forms. Spherical lenses correct general vision issues like nearsightedness and farsightedness, providing uniform prescription across the lens. Toric lenses address astigmatism with varying powers in different orientations, ensuring precise correction. Multifocal lenses offer multiple zones for vision correction, enabling wearers to see clearly at various distances, particularly aiding presbyopia. Each type leverages silicone hydrogel's breathable material, enhancing comfort and eye health.</p></p>
